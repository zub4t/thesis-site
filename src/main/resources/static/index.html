How was the state of the art addressed?

To address the state of the art in indoor localization, an extensive search was conducted using a wide range of search engines. The search focused on a comprehensive set of keywords that are frequently used in the indoor localization world, including.

  • FTM
  • Indoor localization
  • WiFi
  • RTT
  • BLE
  • UWB
  • PDR
  • RSSI
  • Inertial sensors
  • Radio map construction
  • Acoustic
  • Visible light communication
  • Machine learning
  • Computer vision
  • Sensor fusion
  • Magnetic field
  • Ultrasonic
  • Bluetooth
  • ZigBee
  • Hybrid positioning
  • Pedestrian tracking
  • Crowdsourcing
  • LoRa
  • GNSS
  • Dead reckoning
  • Big data
  • Localization algorithms
  • Map matching

The search engines used included Google Scholar, MDPI, IEEE, ACM Digital Library, SpringerLink, ScienceDirect, and Scopus. These search engines were chosen for their ability to provide high-quality academic research and articles related to the field of indoor localization.
